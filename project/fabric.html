Fabric Design System

Creating, scaling and improving H&M Group multibrand design system.

How we designed, developed and scaled a functional, reusable and accessible multibrand design system.

Over the period of 6 months I researched and designed new components for the H&M Group design system. We shipped to the design and front-end community, modern, responsive and accessible components.

A multibrand system for H&M's portoflio brands.

One of the main challenges of building a multibrand design system was ensuring consistency and cohesion across multiple brands, while still allowing each brand to maintain its own unique identity and personality. Constant collaboration and trasparency was key to ensure a better collaboration across brands stakeholders.

I held Figma workshop on Auto Layout, Tokens, Slot Components, Prototyping and more.

Research

I conducted visual audit, pattern inconsistency, while understanding component function in relation to customer needs.

Design

I crafted the visual of the components and patterns toghether with the design tokens to ensure a elegant, pleasant and fashionable aesthethic in line with H&M brand.

Document

I wrote guidelines and best practices so they were easy to understand and use for both designers and developers.

Collaborate

I held workshop, alignment meeting and created educational content to communicate the value of Fabric and to collect constant feedback.
